Saint Stephen

5 AD to 36 AD

The First Martyr and Beacon of Faith

Saint Stephen, the first Christian martyr, exemplifies unwavering faith, compassion, and dedication to Christ. Chosen as a deacon for his wisdom and piety, Stephen served the early Christian community by aiding the poor and preaching the Gospel with fervor. His eloquent defense of Christianity before the Sanhedrin highlighted his deep understanding of Scripture and unflinching commitment to his beliefs.
